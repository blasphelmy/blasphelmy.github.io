Background

I'm from a first generation family of immigrants. My parents had me in Vietnam and we moved here when I was really young. We did struggle a lot but we've managed to do alright for ourselves.

It would be an understatement to say that I was a little lost after graduating high school. I didn't really know what I wanted to do with myself or what any of my future plans are. But after being part of life for a few years, I decided I wanted to go back into school, and since then I've been working really hard on all of my classes. I've come to recognize the opportunities and privileges that I have and are available to me, and I aim to make the most of that.

Work Experiences

- Peer Tutor at Renton Technical College

Aside from being a student, I'm also a tutor for Computer Science over at the Learning Resource and Career Center at RTC as part of my Work Study Program.
